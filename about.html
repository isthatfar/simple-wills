About Us

Properwills is a will writing company helping thousands of individuals and families plan for their future.

Making a will should be jargon-free, affordable and easy. For some it's already quite a big thing – it shouldn't be complicated and expensive on top of that.

Our easy to use online questionnaire takes you through the main choices to make and gathers your answers. We then write and send you a high quality will adapted to your situation. All that in 1-3 days.
